## Who this site is for

People recovering from 7-OH, kratom synthetics, or related opioid dependence —
along with the friends, partners, and family members trying to understand what
that recovery actually looks like.

## How to use this site

- The left sidebar lists every page by category.
- The search bar above is full-text and searches every page on the site.
- If you are in active withdrawal **right now**, go straight to
  [#sos-resources](/active-withdrawal/sos-resources).
