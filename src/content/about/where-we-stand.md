## Who the site is for

Some people use kratom or 7-OH without developing dependence. The site isn't written for them. It's written for people whose use of these compounds went sideways, where dependence on concentrated 7-OH builds quickly and withdrawal hits like classical opioid withdrawal.

The framing across this site is calibrated for that audience. It will sometimes read as one-sided to readers whose experience is different.

## On bans and regulation

The site does not take a stance on bans, schedules, or restrictions on kratom, 7-OH, or any related compound, not for, not against. Nobody pays us, we don't pay anyone, and we have no relationship with regulatory advocacy of any flavor.

The site's focus is recovery. If you came here looking for that content, **[How to Use This Website](/start-here/how-to-use-this-website)** is the map.
